Who We Are

EPKG, based in Shaoxing City’s Shangyu District, is a leading blow molding manufacturer, strategically located 120 km from Ningbo Port and 220 km from Shanghai Port. Since 2012, we’ve crafted high-quality plastic packaging with precision and care.

Our advanced facilities and expert team specialize in R&D, mold design, injection and blow molding, silk-screen printing, hot stamping, and more. Certified under ISO 9001, we ensure every product meets the highest standards.

Driven by integrity, efficiency, and innovation, EPKG partners with global brands to create packaging that elevates their products. Join us in shaping the future of packaging.
